QUERY: Which club has the longest unbeaten run in Serie A history?

EXPECTED:
The club that holds the record for the longest unbeaten streak in Serie A history, and the length of that run.

RESPONSE:
AC Milan holds the record for the longest unbeaten streak in Serie A history, going 58 consecutive matches without defeat from the 1990–91 season to the 1992–93 season. This run started with a 0–0 draw against Parma on 26 May 1991 and ended with a 0–1 loss to Parma on 21 March 1993.
