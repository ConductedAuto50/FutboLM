QUERY: Who was the oldest goal scorer in a World Cup match?

EXPECTED:
Name and age (at the time of scoring) of the oldest player to have scored a goal in a FIFA World Cup match.

RESPONSE:
The oldest goal-scorer in a FIFA World Cup match was Roger Milla from Cameroon. He scored at the age of 42 years and 39 days against Russia in the 1994 World Cup in the USA.
