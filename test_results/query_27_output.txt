QUERY: Who was the youngest player to score in a World Cup final?

EXPECTED:
Name and age (at the time of the final) of the youngest player to score a goal in a FIFA World Cup final.

RESPONSE:
Pele was the youngest player to score in a World Cup final. He was 17 years old when he scored two goals in Brazil's 5-2 victory over Sweden in the 1958 final.

Kylian Mbappe became the youngest player to score in a World Cup final since Pele when he scored at age 19 in France's 4-2 win over Croatia in the 2018 final.
