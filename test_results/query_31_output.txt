QUERY: Who scored the fastest hat-trick in Champions League history?

EXPECTED:
Player's name and the time taken for the fastest hat-trick scored in UEFA Champions League history.

RESPONSE:
Mohamed Salah holds the record for the fastest hat-trick in Champions League history. He scored a treble in just 7 minutes (timed at 6 minutes 12 seconds) for Liverpool against Rangers on 12/10/2022. He achieved this feat after coming off the bench in the match which Liverpool won 7-1.
