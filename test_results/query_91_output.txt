QUERY: Who was the youngest player to score a hat-trick in the Champions League?

EXPECTED:
Name and age (at time of hat-trick) of the youngest player to score a hat-trick in a UEFA Champions League match.

RESPONSE:
The youngest player to score a hat-trick in the UEFA Champions League was Raúl. He achieved this for Real Madrid against Ferencváros on 18 October 1995, at the age of 18 years and 114 days.
