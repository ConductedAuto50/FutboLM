QUERY: Who was the captain of Chelsea during their 2012 Champions League win?

EXPECTED:
Name of the player who captained Chelsea in the 2012 UEFA Champions League final.

RESPONSE:
Frank Lampard was the captain of Chelsea during the 2012 UEFA Champions League final match against Bayern Munich. Although John Terry was the official club captain, he was suspended for the final, and Lampard wore the armband.
